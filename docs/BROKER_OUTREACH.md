## Lead Pricing Matrix

| Credit Tier | Payout Per Lead |
|---|---|
| Excellent + Employed + £35k+ | £30-40 |
| Good + Employed + £20k+ | £25-30 |
| Fair + Any employment | £18-22 |
| Poor (credit builder segment) | £15-18 |

## What Brokers Want

1. **Consent proof** — Screenshot of the consent checkbox (we have this)
2. **Lead freshness** — Leads sold within 24 hours are worth 2x more
3. **Volume consistency** — They want predictable supply
4. **Data quality** — Valid emails, accurate income ranges
5. **Exclusivity** — Some brokers pay more for exclusive leads

## Compliance Checklist for Lead Sales

- [ ] Lead has `consentToShare: true`
- [ ] Data is anonymized before sharing with unvetted partners
- [ ] Partner is FCA-authorized or exempt
- [ ] Lead data is transferred securely (encrypted email or API)
- [ ] Records of consent are retained for 24 months
